# Gold Rush: The Debtor's Escape 🐉

**Gold Rush** is a high-stakes Stealth-Roguelike PWA (Progressive Web App) designed with **Total Digital Inclusion** at its core. Created by **Anderson Carvalho (Eu Concego Jogar)**, this project pushes the boundaries of accessible gaming for the visually impaired, combining complex tactical mechanics with an immersive 3D soundscape.

## 🏹 The Premise

In most games, you enter a dungeon to find treasure. In **Gold Rush**, you have already found it. You start at the very bottom of a perilous labyrinth with a massive debt to pay and a backpack full of gold. 

**The goal? Get out alive.**

Every hit you take, every trap you spring, costs you gold. Will you arrive at the surface as a free man, or will the dungeon claim what is left of your life and your fortune?

## 🛠️ Key Features

- **Tactical Stealth Mechanics:** Inspired by *Tactical Battle*, the game uses a grid-based movement system where **Facing** matters. Sneak behind enemies for a devastating *Sneak Attack* or use the environment to stay out of their sight.
- **Dynamic Soundscape (3D Audio):**
    - **Wind Navigation:** Inspired by *Shadow Line*, a subtle wind sound indicates open paths, allowing for fluid navigation without constant scanning.
    - **Proximity Awareness:** Hear the growls of enemies or the shimmer of loot before you even see them.
    - **Reverb Sneak Attack:** A dramatic audio shift (reverb + whisper) highlights successful stealth takedowns.
- **Deep RPG Systems (D&D 5e SRD):** Attributes, Encumbrance (weight affects noise and speed), and Charisma-based negotiations with mysterious merchants.
- **Progressive World:** From a desperate debtor to a legendary Guild Master. Your reputation grows with every successful escape.
- **Total Accessibility:** Built for NVDA and TalkBack with optimized ARIA regions, "Auto-scan" features, and landscape-oriented split controls for mobile ergonomics.

## 🕹️ Gameplay
- **Left Panel:** Orientation & Utilities (Facing, Inventory, Scan).
- **Center Panel:** Actions (Attack, Get, Skills).
- **Right Panel:** Interactive Grid Board.
